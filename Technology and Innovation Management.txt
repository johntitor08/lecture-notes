Technology: Bilimsel bilginin pratik kullanımıdır.

Innovation: Yeni fikirlerin ekonomik veya sosyal değer yaratacak şekilde uygulanmasıdır.

Invention yeni bir şeyin icadı, innovation ise onun kullanılmasıdır.

Innovation türleri: product, process, business model.

Technology is the application of scientific knowledge to solve practical problems and improve human life.

Innovation is the process of turning new ideas into products, services, or processes that create value.

Invention: creation of a new idea or product.

Innovation: successful implementation of that idea in the market.

Technology provides the tools and knowledge, while innovation uses them to create value and competitive advantage.

Industry 4.0 refers to the fourth industrial revolution where digital technologies transform manufacturing and industry.

Key technologies:
- Artificial Intelligence.
- Internet of Things.
- Automation.
- Big Data.
